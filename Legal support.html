For Parents

Recognize, respond, report

Warning signs

Sudden mood changes, withdrawal, fear of specific places/people, unexplained marks, sleep issues, drop in grades.

How to talk to your child

Ask calm, open questions: “Has anyone ever made you feel uncomfortable?” Believe them, avoid blame, ensure safety first.

If you suspect abuse

Stay calm, document details, seek medical/psych support, and use official reporting channels. Your child’s safety comes first.
