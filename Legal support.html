Legal Support

Your Rights. Your Voice. Your Child's Safety.

ProtectUS connects families with volunteer lawyers who guide them when facing cases of school harassment, abuse, or unsafe environments. Our goal is to help you understand the law, take action safely, and protect your child’s rights.

Free Legal Guidance

Speak privately with a volunteer lawyer about school harassment, sexual abuse, negligence, or unsafe school practices. We help you understand what steps are legally available.

How to Report a Case

We walk you through the correct reporting process: filing complaints, gathering evidence, contacting authorities, and protecting your child during investigations.

Documents & Procedures

Access templates for complaints, school letters, medical reports, and legal declarations to make the process easier and faster.

When should you contact a lawyer?

  • If the school refuses to take your complaint seriously
  • If your child shows signs of physical, emotional, or sexual abuse
  • If you want to understand your rights before reporting
  • If another institution tries to silence or block your claim
