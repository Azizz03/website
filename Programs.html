Programs

Practical actions inside schools

Students 10–14

Boundaries & respect, bystander courage, safe reporting. Games, role-play, stories, and a confidential feedback box.

Teachers & Staff

Recognize signs, respond to disclosures, reporting protocols, and building safe classroom culture.

Parents

Conversations at home, detecting red flags, legal steps, and supporting a child after disclosure.

Legal & case handling

Case intake, evidence guidance, filings, and liaison with child protection authorities. Confidential and child-centred.

Psychological care

Trauma-informed therapy and group support for children and families, with referral pathways where needed.
