For Children

You have the right to be safe.

✅ You can say NO to anything that makes you uncomfortable.
✅ No one has the right to touch you without permission.
✅ Tell a trusted adult — again and again — until someone helps.
✅ Bad secrets make you feel scared or sad; you should tell them.
✅ Online messages can be harmful too — show an adult if unsure.
✅ Your feelings matter. We will believe you.

If you feel unsafe: three steps

1) Say NO
Use a strong voice. You can leave right away.
2) Go away
Go to a safe place with people around (classroom, teacher, office).
3) Tell someone
Talk to your parent, a teacher, the school counselor, or us — now.
