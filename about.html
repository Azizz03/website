Why we exist

We work to make every Tunisian school a safe place to learn. Our team of educators, psychologists, and legal partners deliver prevention, training, and support — aligned with SDG-16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions.

Protection
Zero-tolerance for harassment and abuse in schools.
Awareness
Age-appropriate workshops and parent sensitization.
Justice
Clear reporting pathways and legal accompaniment.
Care
Psychological first aid and trauma-informed care.
